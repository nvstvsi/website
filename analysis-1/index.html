The Real Line

This subject covers the rigorous foundations of single-variable calculus on the real line. Starting from the construction of ℝ, we develop the theory of sequences, series, continuity, differentiability, and Riemann integration.

Planned Chapters

  • 1. The Real Numbers — Construction, completeness, supremum property
  • 2. Sequences and Series — Convergence, Cauchy sequences, absolute convergence
  • 3. Limits and Continuity — ε-δ definitions, properties, uniform continuity
  • 4. Differentiation — Derivatives, mean value theorems, L'Hôpital's rule
  • 5. The Riemann Integral — Partitions, integrability, properties
  • 6. The Fundamental Theorem — FTC I & II, integration techniques
